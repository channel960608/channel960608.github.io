Find Chenluo Month

Question：

As we all know, a lot of funny definitions exist in the field of Mathematics and Computer Science. Take it for an
example, Narcissistic number, or pluperfect digital invariant, is defined as a positive integer that equals to the sum
of the powers of its digits: Since 153 = 1 1 1 + 5 5 5 + 3 3 3, 153 can be identified as a Narcissistic number.

Based on the opinion mentioned above, we proposed a brand new and interesting noun ‘Chenluo Month’, which is a month
with this interesting feather: the 1st to 7th of this month are Monday to Sunday respectively. In other words, the
month where the first day is Monday can be defined as Chenluo Month.

In further, we propose the following question: since July 1, 2019 is Monday, the July in 2019 is Chenluo Month. But how
can we find the next July which is Chenluo Month as well, or can we find all Chenluo Month presented through a formula?
